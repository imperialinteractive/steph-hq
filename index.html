Turkey & Avocado Rice Bowl

Lumen recipe · ~480 cal · 35g protein · 38g carbs

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 100g ground turkey, cooked
  • ½ cup brown rice, cooked
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • ½ cup sliced cucumber
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt, pepper, fresh herbs (parsley or cilantro)

Method

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. Brown ground turkey in a pan over medium-high heat with salt and pepper until cooked through.
  3. Layer rice in a bowl, top with turkey, avocado, and cucumber.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice, finish with fresh herbs.
